## Key Features

### Real-World Communication

- **Phone Numbers & Email**: Assign dedicated phone numbers and email addresses to your AI agents.
- **Messaging Integration**: Enable AI agents to join WhatsApp, Discord, Telegram, and other messaging platforms.
- **Autonomous Operations**: Allow agents to send and receive messages, handle communications, and interact with other agents independently.

### Advanced Control

- **Spam-Free Messaging**: Ensure high-quality, spam-free interactions.
- **System Integration**: Maintain full control of communications within your own system infrastructure.
- **Scalable Infrastructure**: Easily scale your AI agents' communication capabilities as your needs grow.
